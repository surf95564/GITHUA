## Getting Started

### First Launch
When you first open the app, you'll be presented with two options:
- **Sign In**: If you already have an account
- **Create Account**: If you're a new user

### System Requirements
- Android 6.0 (API level 24) or higher
- Minimum 100MB free storage space
- Active internet connection
- GPS enabled (for location-based features)

## User Accounts

### Creating an Account
1. Tap **Create Account**
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password
4. Enter your full name
5. Add your phone number
6. Tap **Sign Up**
7. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

### Logging In
1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Tap **Sign In**
4. Optional: Check "Remember me" to stay signed in

### Resetting Password
1. On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Use your new password to log in

### Managing Your Profile
1. Open the menu and tap **My Profile**
2. Edit your information:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Profile picture
3. Tap **Save Changes**

## Browsing Items

### Search and Filter
1. On the home screen, tap the **Search** icon
2. Enter keywords (e.g., "laptop", "camera")
3. Use filters to narrow results:
   - **Category**: Electronics, Furniture, Tools, etc.
   - **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum daily rate
   - **Rating**: View highly-rated items
   - **Location**: Items near you
4. Sort results by:
   - Most relevant
   - Price (low to high)
   - Price (high to low)
   - Highest rated
   - Newest listings

### Viewing Item Details
1. Tap on any item to view full details
2. Check:
   - Full description
   - Photo gallery
   - Daily, weekly, and monthly prices
   - Availability calendar
   - Owner information
   - Reviews and ratings
   - Condition and location

### Adding to Favorites
- Tap the **heart icon** on any item to add it to your favorites
- Access favorites from the menu

## Making a Rental

### Creating a Booking
1. Find the item you want to rent
2. Tap **Rent Now**
3. Select dates:
   - Start date
   - End date
4. Choose delivery method:
   - **Pickup**: Collect from owner
   - **Delivery**: Owner delivers to you
5. Review the quote (daily rate × number of days)
6. Tap **Continue**

### Selecting Payment Method
1. Choose payment method:
   - Credit/Debit card
   - Digital wallet (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
   - Bank transfer
2. Add payment details if new
3. Review total amount and fees
4. Tap **Pay Now**

### Booking Confirmation
1. Review confirmation details:
   - Item details
   - Rental period
   - Total cost
   - Pickup/delivery details
2. Tap **Confirm Booking**
3. You'll receive a confirmation email
4. Owner will be notified and may accept/decline

## Managing Rentals

### Viewing Active Rentals
1. Open menu and tap **My Rentals**
2. Select **Active** tab
3. View all current rentals with:
   - Item name and image
   - Rental dates
   - Days remaining
   - Owner contact
   - Return instructions

### Tracking Rental Status
- **Pending**: Awaiting owner confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Ready for pickup/delivery
- **In Progress**: Rental is active
- **Completed**: Rental finished
- **Cancelled**: Rental was cancelled

### Communicating with Owner
1. From rental details, tap **Message Owner**
2. Type your message
3. Tap **Send**
4. Owner notifications help with:
   - Delivery coordination
   - Extension requests
   - Questions about the item

### Extending a Rental
1. Open the active rental
2. Tap **Request Extension**
3. Select new end date
4. Review updated cost
5. Submit request
6. Wait for owner approval

### Returning Items
1. When rental ends, contact owner
2. Follow return instructions
3. Ensure item is in same condition as received
4. Take photos if requested
5. Confirm return with owner

## Payments

### Payment Methods
The app supports:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Bank transfers (in some regions)

### Adding Payment Method
1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment Methods**
2. Tap **Add Payment Method**
3. Select payment type
4. Enter payment details
5. Tap **Save**

### Viewing Payment History
1. Open menu → **Payment History**
2. See all transactions with:
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Item name
   - Status
   - Receipt link

### Refunds
- Cancellations within 48 hours: Full refund
- Cancellations 48 hours before: 70% refund
- Cancellations within 24 hours: 50% refund
- After rental starts: No refund (unless owner approves)

## Reviews

### Writing a Review
1. After rental completion, tap **Leave Review**
2. Rate the item (1-5 stars)
3. Rate the owner (1-5 stars)
4. Write detailed comment (optional)
5. Share if rental is suitable for others
6. Tap **Submit Review**

### Reading Reviews
1. Open item details
2. Scroll to **Reviews** section
3. See rating distribution
4. Read individual reviews
5. Filter by rating or date

### Managing Your Reviews
1. Go to **My Reviews**
2. Edit or delete your reviews
3. Only edit within 30 days of posting

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
**Problem**: Can't log in
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- Reset password if you forgot it
- Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → Rental App → Storage → Clear Cache
- Reinstall the app if issues persist

### Booking Not Going Through
**Problem**: Booking fails at payment
- **Solution**: 
  - Check internet connection
  - Verify payment method has sufficient funds
  - Try a different payment method
  - Contact support if issue persists

### Messages Not Sending
**Problem**: Can't send messages to owner
- **Solution**:
  - Ensure internet connection is active
  - Check if owner account is still active
  - Restart the app
  - Try again in a few minutes

### Location Not Updating
**Problem**: Map shows wrong location
- **Solution**:
  - Enable GPS in device settings
  - Allow app location permissions
  - Close and reopen the app
  - Restart your device

### Payment Issues
**Problem**: Payment declined
- **Solution**:
  - Check card details are correct
  - Ensure card isn't expired
  - Contact your bank about transaction blocks
  - Try a different payment method

### Cant Find Item I Want to Rent
**Problem**: Item no longer available
- **Solution**:
  - Item may have been rented
  - Contact owner via message
  - Check similar items in same category
  - Set up favorites to track new items
